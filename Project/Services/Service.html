Amankaya Plan

The Amankaya Plan is one of the best for university students because it takes you to visit a festival and enjoy it. First, you will visit the International Flower and Coffee Fair. Then our plan will take you to the Panama Jungle Wildlife Refuge. Additionally, college students will have the option to do their social work during the trip.Our plan includes transportation and lodging at a cost of $100. In short, with our plan, you will be able to visit festivals and places in the province of Chiriqui, making it one of the best travel options for university students.
