International Flower and Coffee Fair







It is located in Boquete. It is a place that has flowers such as orchids, roses, and chavelitas. The person who visits this international fair can buy a variety of flowers and the national coffee that is made in this same province. The Flower and Coffee Fair is open throughout the year, organizing and planning the fair event that takes place every year in January. With our amankaya plan you can go at a price of 100 dollars.

Monkey Festival in Bijao







It is located in San Andres (District of Bugaba). The festival lasts one day and is held every year in July. In addition to the opportunity to taste these delicacies of Chiriqui food, the Monkey Festival in Bijao offers visitors a varied presentation of folkloric dance groups from various schools and towns in the province of Chiriqui and other provinces. With our amankaya plan you can go at a price of 100 dollars.
